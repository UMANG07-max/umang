Today, I am a proud student of Class 9 – Nurture Batch at Allen Career Institute, Kota, Rajasthan, one of India's most prestigious coaching institutes for engineering aspirants. I study at the renowned Sammunnat Building, where I am rigorously preparing for the IIT JEE 2030 examination — the gateway to India's most elite engineering institutions. Currently residing in the vibrant student hub of Talwandi, Kota (PIN: 340023), I wake up every day driven by a singular goal: to earn my seat at an IIT.
